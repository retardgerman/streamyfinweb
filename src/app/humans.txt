/* ABOUT */
Streamyfin is a mobile Jellyfin client available for Android and iOS, designed to enhance your media streaming experience. Built with user-friendly principles, it offers offline access, a customizable interface, and much more.

We believe in open-source values and community engagement, welcoming feedback and contributions from users to continually improve the app.
